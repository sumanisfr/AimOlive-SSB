OBJECTIVE: AIMOLIVE

AimOlive was forged from a simple realization: while the will to serve the nation is abundant, high-quality, accessible resources for SSB psychological testing are scarce.

This platform is not a commercial academy. It is a specialized digital arena designed to sharpen the perceptual and creative reflexes of defence aspirants through rigorous, time-bound simulations.

TACTICAL ADVANTAGES

  • COMBAT-REALISTIC TIMING

    Our automated timers (30s observation / 4m writing) exactly replicate the pressure of the actual SSB testing environment, building your mental endurance.

  • CENTRALIZED INTEL ARMORY

    Stop searching. Access a massive, organized library of OIR, PPDT, TAT, WAT, SRT, and GTO materials in one secure location.

  • ZERO-FRICTION PRIVACY

    No logins. No tracking. No fees. Your practice data is yours alone. Train immediately, anytime, anywhere.
