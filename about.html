About AimOlive

What is AimOlive?

AimOlive is a non-profit platform created by Suman Kumar Ghosh to help SSB aspirants improve their PPDT and TAT story writing skills through time-bound, self-paced practice sessions.

It aims to build the qualities of observation, imagination, and decision-making ‚Äî the essential traits of an officer. This platform doesn‚Äôt provide coaching or tips; it provides the arena for you to practice, evaluate, and improve yourself.

Our Mission & Benefits

This project is built on the belief that disciplined self-assessment is the key to success. Our mission is to provide a free, accessible, and realistic tool for every defence aspirant.

  • Comprehensive Library: Access a huge, organized collection of all SSB materials (OIR, TAT, WAT, SRT, GTO) in one place, saving you time.
  • Realistic Simulation: Practice PPDT & TAT with time constraints that mimic the real exam, training your mind to perform under pressure.
  • 100% Free & Open: No logins, no fees, no subscriptions. This platform is built for the community, by a member of the community.
  • Self-Paced Learning: Practice anytime, anywhere. Review your own stories and track your improvement without external pressure.
