PPDT Practice SOP

Standard Operating Procedure for Online Drills

PHASE 1: OBSERVATION PROTOCOL

Once you initiate a test sequence, a hazy picture will be displayed on your screen. A countdown timer will immediately begin.

  • Duration: You have exactly 30 seconds to observe the image.
  • Objective: Identify the number of characters, their age, gender, mood, and the central action/theme.
  • Auto-Termination: The image will automatically vanish when the timer hits zero.
PHASE 2: REPORT WRITING

Immediately after observation, the writing phase begins. You must type your story in the provided text area or write it on paper offline.

  • Duration: You have 4 minutes to complete your story.
  • Objective: Write a logical story with a clear Past (what led to this), Present (current action), and Future (outcome).
  • Integrity Check: Be honest with the time limit. If you cannot finish in 4 minutes, practice more to increase your speed.
TACTICAL NOTE: To restart any mission instantly, simply refresh the page. Data is not saved between sessions to ensure fresh practice every time.
